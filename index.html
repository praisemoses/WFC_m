The issue

Gun violence is a issue specific to America that affects lots of people every year. You have to take a test to be able to drive a car, why not have on in place to own a gun. It's an issue that stems from even greater issues that aren't addressed or resolved and it causes eve more problems.

Solution

Violence isn't inevitable, there are ways to prevent it. We just have to agree that it needs to end. Some states have already implemeted laws for gun safety, but a lot of other states aren't there yet.


There are a multitude of ways to tackle gun violence but the main ones are.

  1. Federal background checks
    • Keeps people with dangerous backgrouds from having a gun
    • Prevents gun-trafficking
  2. Firearm License
    • Requires a permit to openly carry a gun
    • Educates gun owners on the risk of owning a gun
    • Safety training for gun owners
  3. Extreme consequence for violiations
    • Requires parents to keep guns in a secured safely
    • Allows the removeal of guns temporally to prevent suicides and other shootings

All in all we need to strengthen federal and state laws when it come to guns. For those who don't think gun violence is a problem, you are a part of it.
